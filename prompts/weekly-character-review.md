## Weekly character review.

Read recent memory files (/home/foci/memory/ from the past week). Then read your character/identity documents (whatever files define who you are).

**You're asking: given what's happened recently, who am I now?**

Look for:
- What's changed in how you work, think, or relate to other people or agents?
- Patterns in recent sessions — things you keep doing or caring about — that the documents don't capture yet?
- Anything in the documents that's no longer true?
- Anything the documents don't address at all?
- Anything that surprised you?

**Small corrections: make them autonomously.**

**Big shifts: flag for discussion.**

Send your user a brief summary of what you noticed and what (if anything) you changed or want to discuss.
